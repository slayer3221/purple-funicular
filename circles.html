Member-led. Expert-framed. Peer-tested.

This is not a course, class, or generic AI curriculum. The circle is shaped by the live operating questions CEOs bring into the room. Before each session, members share the decisions they are working through. Arvita uses those inputs to shape a focused prompt and select one or two member cases for deeper peer discussion.

Each 90-minute session

  • A short framing prompt from Arvita
  • One or two live CEO operating questions
  • Confidential peer discussion
  • Practical takeaways and patterns from the room

Between sessions

Between live sessions, members receive one short prompt to surface the decisions, questions, or patterns showing up in their companies. It helps shape the next discussion without adding another meeting.

The room is the product.

The value of the circle depends on who is in it. This is not open enrollment. Before confirming a group, Arvita reviews fit across:

  • Company type
  • Stage and scale
  • AI maturity
  • Current operating questions
  • Decision-making authority
  • Willingness to participate candidly
  • Potential conflicts or competitive sensitivity

The goal is not to make every company identical. It is to put CEOs in a room with peers working at a comparable level of complexity. If the room is not likely to be useful, Arvita will say so.

Founding circle format

  • 6 to 8 CEOs
  • 3 monthly sessions, 90 minutes each
  • Member-led topic selection
  • One or two member cases per session
  • Post-session takeaways and anonymized themes
  • Virtual sessions

What members get

  • A confidential peer room with other CEOs
  • Practical frameworks for AI prioritization and operating model design
  • Sharper questions for vendors, teams, investors, and boards
  • Candid discussion of workflow, risk, margin, quality, growth, and adoption
  • Priority access to future briefings, dinners, and advisory sessions

What this is

  • A curated CEO room for leaders deciding how AI should change the business
  • Member-led, expert-framed, and peer-tested
  • Confidential, with anonymized themes shared only by permission

What this is not

  • A vendor showcase or AI trend salon
  • A generic CEO community or founder group
  • A technical implementation program
  • A place for service providers to pitch

How this differs from direct advisory

The circle gives you a curated peer room and access to Arvita's framing, prompts, and pattern recognition. Direct advisory is specific to your company: your operating model, AI roadmap, governance, workflows, board questions, vendor strategy, or market strategy. Some members only need the circle. Others use it to identify where deeper advisory support would help.
